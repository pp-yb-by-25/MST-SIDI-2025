5. Conditionals (Types 0, 1, 2, 3 – Complex Sentences)

    Type 0 (general truth): If you ___mix____ (mix) red and blue, you __get_____ (get) purple.

    Type 1 (real possibility): If she __studies_____ (study) hard, she ___will pass____ (pass) the exam with flying colors.

    Type 2 (hypothetical present/future): If I ___won____ (win) the lottery, I __would travel_____ (travel) around the world.

    Type 3 (hypothetical past): If they ___had left____ (leave) earlier, they __would have avoided_____ (avoid) the traffic jam.

    Mixed conditional: If I ___had took____ (take) that job last year, I ___would have been____ (be) happier now.
